On the occasion of the kickoff of our SAKINA project, we would like to provide an overview of our ongoing biodiversity projects.

### BINaRi | Biodiversity Indicators for Sustainability Reporting

In BINaRi, we are developing scientifically sound biodiversity indicators (e.g. tree species composition, ecosystem condition) to support companies in meeting EU reporting requirements under CSRD and ESRS E4.
Access is provided via a web dashboard or an API.
The project is funded by Push! Hessen.

### SAKINA | Satellite Data-Based AI for Sustainable Reporting

SAKINA is a cooperation project with the University of Kassel, Department of Environmentally Sound Products and Processes.
Together, we are conducting a feasibility study on automating the preparation of sustainability reports in accordance with CSRD/ESRS, with a particular focus on environmental factors (ESRS Environment E).
The project is funded under the Innovation Programme for Business Models and Pioneer Solutions (IGP).

### AI-Biodynamics | Artificial Intelligence for Biodiversity Mapping and Decision-Making in Nature Conservation

AI-Biodynamics is a cooperative project involving German and South Korean partners.
The project aims to develop a scalable solution that replaces fragmented and labor-intensive monitoring practices by integrating satellite remote sensing and citizen science data.
It is funded by the BMFTR.
